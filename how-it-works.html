Our 4-Step Repair Loop

We make repairing your devices fast, safe, and completely stress-free. Review our systematic diagnostic checkups, honest transparent quotes, microscope soldering, and warranty details.

01

Advanced System Diagnostics Scan

Diagnostic Fee: ₹0 (Completely Free)
Average Duration: 15 - 45 Minutes

Every laptop or mobile checked into our lab undergoes a comprehensive multi-point diagnostic inspection. Using digital multimeters, oscilloscopes, and thermal diagnostic cameras, our engineers trace critical power rails, check circuit impedance, analyze heat signatures, and inspect motherboard conditions to isolate the exact root cause of the failure, rather than just treating visible symptoms.

  • Thermal imaging to identify shorted capacitors instantly
  • Diagnostic checks for battery health and SSD storage sectors
  • Isolate exact component failures with zero guesswork
02

Honest Rework Quotation

Cost Guarantee: No Hidden Fees
Delivery Format: Phone call & WhatsApp

Transparency is key to our business model. Once diagnostics are complete, we calculate an upfront cost bracket based on genuine OEM parts costs and transparent labor fees. We contact you via phone call or WhatsApp to share a detailed breakdown of the diagnostics, the required spares, and repair timelines. We only initiate physical repairs after obtaining your explicit pre-approval.

  • Multi-option estimates (OEM genuine vs high-quality budget)
  • 100% pre-authorized work before tools touch the hardware
  • Complete protection under our 'No Fix, No Fee' guarantee
03

Micro-Soldering & Stress Tests

Equipment Level: Stereo Microscopes
Stress Test Loop: 3 Hours Benchmarks

After obtaining approval, your device is transferred to our specialized dust-free rework benches. For chip-level board errors, our micro-soldering experts work under stereo microscopes to replace micro-capacitors, re-solder PMICs, re-flash BIOS chips, or restore liquid-corroded traces. Once the hardware repair is finished, the device is subjected to a rigorous 3-hour stress-testing loop to guarantee absolute stability.

  • Microscope-guided precision re-soldering and IC replacement
  • 3-hour benchmark testing (CPU loading, thermal cycling, I/O checks)
  • Premium thermal compound application to restore heat dissipation
